Biography

Siwen Zheng is a master student in the School of Computer Science, at Hangzhou Dianzi University(HDU). She revevied Bachelor of Engineering from Liaoning Shihua University in June, 2016. She is now doing research in the Lab of LOT group under the supervision of Prof. Jianhui Zhang in HDU.

Her research focuses on Machine Learning, Mobile and Sensor Networks,Crowdsourcing, Game Theory, etc.
